Inline math is useful: $A + B + \underbrace{C + D}_{\small\text{Floppy disk!}}$.

Monospaced math: $\texttt{char} \subset \texttt{int} \subset \texttt{long}$.

But centred equations are also fun!

$$
\begin{align}
x + y + z &= 1 \\\\
2x - y + 3z &= 2 \\\\
4x - 5y + 10z &= 3 \\\\
\end{align}
$$
