title: Reverse Engineering
---

Reverse engineering is the process of gleaning implementation details, typically from a black box. It is also a category of {% tag "CTF", "ctf" %} challenge which involves tasks such as decompiling and understanding program logic. Reverse is also one of the strongest UNO cards, extremely effective in certain situations.
